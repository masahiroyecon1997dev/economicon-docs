---
title: Econometrics Glossary
description: A quick A-Z glossary for terms such as BLUE, consistency, unbiasedness, R^2, and IV.
sidebar:
  order: 2
---

- BLUE: Best Linear Unbiased Estimator.
- Consistency: As sample size grows, the estimator converges to the true value.
- Endogeneity: Correlation between explanatory variables and the error term.
- GMM: Generalized Method of Moments.
- IV: Instrumental Variables.
- OLS: Ordinary Least Squares.
- R^2: Coefficient of determination.
- Unbiasedness: Expected value of estimator equals true parameter.
